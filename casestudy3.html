The website is accessible to the public eye. However there is also a unique feature not many other know.

The Yale School of Art website can be edited by any student who currently goes to the school, whether it be the images in the background or text displayed in the primary sections. As a result, the website primarily caters to the student demographic, providing them with the latest news and updates about events and activities.
